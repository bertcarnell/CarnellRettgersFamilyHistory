Time Line

  • 20 Dec 1860, SC secedes
  • 19 Jan 1861, GA secedes
  • 12 Apr 1861, Shots at Ft Sumter
  • Early 1862, GA calls up volunteer regiments and 48th GA Volunteer Infantry is formed
  • Seven Days Campaign
    • 26 June  1862 Battle of Beaver Dam Creek
    • 1 Jul 1862 Battle of Malvern Hill
  • 30 Aug 1862 Battle of 2nd Manassas
  • 17 Sept 1862, Battle of Sharpsburg (Antietam)
  • 13 Dec 1862, Fredericksburg (minimal action for the 48th)
  • 1 May 1863, Chancellorsville
  • 2 July 1863, Gettysburg
  • 30 July 1864, Petersburg, the Battle of the Crater
