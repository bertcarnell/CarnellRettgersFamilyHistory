57th Georgia Infantry Regiment

Time Line

  • 20 Dec 1860, SC secedes
  • 19 Jan 1861, GA secedes
  • 12 Apr 1861, Shots at Ft Sumter
  • Early 1862, GA calls up volunteer regiments and 57th GA Volunteer Infantry is formed
  • 8 Oct 1862, Battle of Perryville, KY (57th GA is in reserve)
  • 16 May 1863, Baker's Creek (Champion Hill)
  • 18 May 1863, Siege of Vicksburg
  • 4 Jul 1863, Surrendered at Vicksburg
  • 12 Sep 1863, Paroled
  • Remnants of the 57th existed through the rest of the war without major engagements.
